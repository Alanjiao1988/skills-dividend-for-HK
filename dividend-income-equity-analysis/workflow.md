# Workflow

Use this workflow when reviewing a dividend-paying listed company.

## Step 1: Classification

Collect company name, ticker, exchange, legal domicile, reporting currency, dividend currency, and listing structure. Classify the security as local company, H-share, red-chip, ADR, REIT, BDC, MLP, fund, trust, ETF, or ordinary company.

## Step 2: Dividend Record

Build a five-year dividend table with ordinary DPS, special DPS, currency, payout ratio, cash-flow payout ratio, and year-on-year growth.

## Step 3: Capital Allocation

Review board and management comments on dividends, buybacks, leverage, reinvestment, acquisitions, and shareholder returns.

## Step 4: Coverage

Review revenue, profit, operating cash flow, capex, free cash flow, net debt, interest cover, maturity wall, and credit quality.

## Step 5: Outlook

Build bear, base, and bull cases for the next three fiscal years. Link dividend forecasts to cash flow, payout policy, leverage, capex, and business outlook.
